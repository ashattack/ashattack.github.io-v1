Tinkerbells Quirks

Tinkerbell is unlike her native Dingo friends since she has been raised American. Shes not a crazy Australian as one would assume. Tinkerbell is afraid of men with beards, cats, vacuums, and her own reflection.

How Tinkerbell Came into My Life

One day, back in 2006, my Dad went with a friend to an animal shelter so she could adopt a dog. My dad wasn't interested in getting another pet as we already had a dog, cat and bunny at the house. But then he saw a dog sitting patiently in her crate, not barking unlike the rest of the orphans. He decided she was the one, his dog soul mate if you will.
